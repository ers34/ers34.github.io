All the perks of pet ownership without all the commitment… no leash attached.

At TheraPup our passion for pets always goes the extra mile for our users to experience a taste of pet ownership. We understand that most people are in situations where they cannot be a pet owner so we’ve created a way to experience a pet’s love and care through a therapeutic scheduling method without any long term commitment.

How It Works

We connect you with your local shelter and pair you with a furry friend that you can spend time with from 15 mins to 1 hour. You get to enjoy their company, help them socialize, and relieve some of the stress that shelters already have to deal with. All we ask for in return is a small donation to that shelter.

The TheraPup Team

Our team understands the therapeutic effects a pet has on an owner and we want to help share that love and care with those who do not have the opportunity to have a pet in their own household.

We work directly with local shelters to relieve the burden of caring for so many animals and we are dedicated to helping people and pups alike.
